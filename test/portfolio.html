Instructor at SkewCode

Hi! I am one of the instructors at SkewCode, where I deliver workshops and conduct bootcamps on various topics, primarily front-end web development.

If you are interested in learning about web technologies like HTML, CSS, JS, jQuery, Bootstrap, Angular and Node.js then do drop a line. We at SkewCode, will be happy to respond to your questions.

Who am I?

I and a full-stack web developer and corporate trainer. With over 10 years of experience in industry and academia, I have a good mix of skills required for training as well as development.

What am I good at?

As a corporate trainer, I deliver trainings to corporate clients as well as working professionals. Each and every course is hands-on intensive andis designed to ensure you start contributing to projects from Day 1.

I do basic designs for websites and develop them. If you got an idea, I can bring it to fruition by building a web app around it.

Need to get in touch?

For more information on the courses, please visit SkewCode website, or drop a line at support@skewcode.com.
